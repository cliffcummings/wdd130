Overview

Purpose

Build revenues for your new/existing business by promoting the Best White Water Rafting Expeirence on the Planet!

Help find new customers

Audience

Adventure-seeking white water enthusiasts.

Who are the target customers?

  • Mid-teens to early 40's
  • Those who want a fun, arenaline rush.
  • Outdoorsy individuals
  • People who want to challenge their skills while rafting

What do customers expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

  • Cost
  • Location
  • Transportation
  • Travel lodging
  • Required liability forms

How will our audience usually access this site?

  • The young crowd will look on their mobile devices for more information
  • The serious cutomer will use a laptop or desktop to do deep-dive research into the rafting possibilities
